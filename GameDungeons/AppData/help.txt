Q: How can I move my hero? 
A: Use the arrow keys

Q: How can I collect an item?
A: They're collected automatically

Q: How can I equip/use an item?
A: Move the mouse over any non-empty 
cell and then left-click

Q: How can I fight the enemy?
A: Everytime you move your hero

Q: How can I win?
A: You have to defeat all the enemies
But beware, you are not immortal!
